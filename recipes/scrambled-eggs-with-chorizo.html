Scrambled Eggs with Chorizo

Description

This chorizo and egg dish is an appetizing breakfast with spicy flavor. My Mexican husband taught me this simple and delicious recipe that serves 2 people, and I use no more than 6 eggs for both of us. You might use less or more — it doesn't matter.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • ¼ cup Mexican-style chorizo, or more to taste
  • 6 large eggs
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Grease a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; warm over medium-high heat. Cook and stir chorizo in the hot skillet until browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Whisk together eggs and salt in a medium bowl until combined; pour over chorizo in the skillet; cook and stir until eggs are set, about 5 minutes.
